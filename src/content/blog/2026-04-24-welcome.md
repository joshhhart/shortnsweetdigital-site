This site is the public-facing end of a content pipeline I'm building in the
open. Every weekday morning, the pipeline picks a keyword, drafts a post,
records an audio version, runs a 19-point SEO audit, and publishes the result
here.

The posts themselves are honest reviews, comparisons, and playbooks for running
a lean marketing agency on one platform. If that sounds useful, stick around.
